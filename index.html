About Winter Arc

Winter Arc is a challenge-first app blending real-life cold-edge missions with on-chain reward mechanics. Complete tasks (cold shower, run, haircut, etc.), validate your completion and earn $WTR. Track profile, timeline, levels and stats — all inside Wapp.

Wapp — The App

Mission feed, profile, levels, timeline and social features. Record completion, gain XP, climb leaderboards.

$WTR — The Token
