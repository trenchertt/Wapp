What is Winter Arc?

Winter Arc is a challenge-driven app where users complete real-life “Winter Arc” missions — cold showers, run, radical haircut, outdoor exposure and more — and earn $WTR tokens as rewards. Track your profile, levels, timeline and statistics. Social and competitive features will appear as the project evolves.

Tasks

Daily and weekly missions of different difficulties — physical, mental, lifestyle. Complete, record, claim tokens.

Profile & Stats

Personal profile, levels, timelines and performance graphs — see your progression over time.

Claim & Use

Earn $WTR for tasks, later claim/withdraw or use within the ecosystem (details to come).

Winter Arc Coin — $WTR

$WTR is the in-app reward token associated with Winter Arc. Tokens are earned for verified mission completions and can be claimed within the app. Tokenomics and distribution are designed to reward activity and community growth.
